## 📌 MOST IMPORTANT CONCEPTS (If you only have 30 min)

1. **Big O Notation** — O(1), O(log n), O(n), O(n log n), O(n²)
2. **Linked List** — singly, insert, delete, traverse
3. **Stack** — LIFO, push/pop, infix→postfix conversion
4. **Queue** — FIFO, enqueue/dequeue
5. **Bubble Sort** — O(n²), stable
6. **Merge Sort** — O(n log n), divide & conquer, stable
7. **Quick Sort** — O(n log n) avg, pivot, in-place
8. **BST** — left < root < right, insert/search/delete
9. **BFS vs DFS** — queue vs stack
10. **Hashing** — hash function, collision resolution

## 💡 VIVA GOLDEN RULES

- Always say **time complexity** for every algorithm you mention
- Always explain **real-world use** (browser history → linked list, etc.)
- Know the **difference** between similar structures (Stack vs Queue, Array vs Linked List)
